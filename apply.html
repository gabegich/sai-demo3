What You'll Receive

Train. Study. Both.

When you join SAI, you don't trade one ambition for another. You step into a structure built so elite sport and a real Bar-Ilan degree can coexist — with the people, time, and money to make both work.

01

Academic Flexibility

Exams deferred around training camps and competition. Assignments restructured for a season abroad. An extended degree timeline if your athletic career calls for it. You set the schedule your sport needs — the academic program adapts.

02

Scholarship Support

Tuition coverage scaled to your competitive level — partial for high-performance athletes, maximum for Olympic and Paralympic competitors. Training stipends and equipment grants where the gap is greatest.

Scales with track placement
03

A Dedicated Coordinator

One person inside Bar-Ilan whose job is the bridge between your sport and your degree. They handle the deferrals, the missed lectures, the catch-up tutoring, the registrar conversations — so you don't.

04

A Real Bar-Ilan Degree

Not athletics-lite. Not a watered-down track. The same degree, the same standards, the same opening of doors when sport ends. SAI doesn't lower the bar — it gives you the structure to clear it on your timeline.

05

A Cohort of Peers

You won't be the only person on campus juggling national-team selection and a final exam. SAI is a community of athletes solving the same problem — and a network of Olympic alumni who already navigated it.

06

A Life After Sport

Career coaching, alumni introductions, internships timed around your competition calendar. SAI is the structural answer to "what's next" — built so the day after your last race isn't the day you start from zero.

Track A · Olympic & Paralympic
For national-team and international competitors
Maximum scholarship, full academic flexibility, dedicated coordinator, extended degree timeline. Built around the demands of an elite competition calendar.
Track B · High Performance
For rising national-level athletes
Partial scholarship, structured academic accommodations, dual-identity coaching. A pathway to Track A as your competitive level rises.
Athletic Excellence
We look for athletes competing at the national team level or above in any Olympic or Paralympic discipline. International results and rankings strengthen your application.
Academic Commitment
You don't need a perfect academic record — you need a genuine commitment to earning a degree alongside your sport. Tell us your field of interest and your plan.
What Happens Next
We review every application within 2 weeks. Shortlisted athletes will be invited for a conversation with the SAI team before a final decision is made.
